Payette Lake

In McCall Idaho

I have played on Payette lake since my first trip to Camp Morrison, whose water-front is on the Eastern Shore. There I was introduced to canoeing and kayaking. Just recently I went on a High Adventure Trip with my quorum and we did some white water rafting, hiking, and jet skiiing. White water rafting and jet skiing are now my two favorite activities on the water.

Things to do at Payette lake

  • Motor boating
  • Water skiing
  • Jetskiing
  • Innertubing
  • kayaking
  • canoeing

What if I don't own a watercraft or vehicle?

  1. Mile High Marina
  2. Cheap Thrills

  4. Backwoods Adventures
From McCall Chamber of Commerce
What is so special about McCall, so unique, so coveted? Payette Lake! Payette Lake is our gem, our jewel in the mountains - a pristine alpine lake to enjoy to your hearts content! You can swim, sail, water-ski, jet-ski, canoe, or kayak; we all share in our love of this beautiful lake! In addition, there are many hiking trails leading to hidden high mountain glacial lakes that will take your breath away! And don't forget the Payette River! Got water sports on your mind?
